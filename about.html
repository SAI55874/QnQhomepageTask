About Us

Our History

We were founded in 2018 by a team of experienced doctors who shared a vision of developing breakthrough medicines to treat some of the world's most serious diseases. As we all know 60% of the healthcare expenditure is only for medicines and healthcare consumables. Despite best efforts from doctors and hospital managements to ensure purchase of quality medicines for patient care, the CDSCO 2016 Report shows evidence of spurious and counterfeit medicines in the Indian Drug market, even amongst branded medicines.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality assured generic medicines at an Affordable price with sustainable business model.
